# SQL Data Analytics Project

This repository demonstrates advanced SQL techniques for analyzing business data and generating actionable insights. Building on data warehousing and exploratory data analysis (EDA), the project focuses on solving real-world business problems through complex SQL queries and analytical reporting.This project highlights practical SQL skills commonly used by data analysts and business intelligence professionals to transform structured data into valuable business insights.

# 🚀 Project Objectives

### Business Analytics (Data Analytics)

### Objective

Develop SQL-based analytical solutions that answer real business questions by exploring datasets, measuring business performance, identifying trends, and building analytical reports.

# 📖 Project Workflow

The project follows a structured analytics workflow:

1. Create and restore the analytical database.
2. Explore the database structure.
3. Explore dimensions and measures.
4. Analyze dates and business metrics.
5. Perform trend analysis.
6. Calculate cumulative metrics.
7. Evaluate business performance.
8. Perform part-to-whole analysis.
9. Segment customers and products.
10. Rank business entities.
11. Build reusable customer and product reports.
